Mitsubishi Lancer Evo 6

(2001)

The Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution VI (Evo 6), produced from 1999 to 2001, is a high-performance, rally-bred sedan that is part of the iconic Evolution series. It was developed for the World Rally Championship (WRC) Group A homologation and is highly sought after by enthusiasts as it was the last Evo produced under strict homologation rules.

  • Engine : 2.0L DOHC 16-valve turbocharged inline-4 (4G63T)
  • Power Output (Official) : 280 PS (276 bhp) at 6,500 rpm
  • Torque : 373 Nm (275 lb-ft) at 3,000 rpm
  • Transmission : 5-speed manual
  • Drivetrain : Full-time All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
  • 0-60 mph Time Approximately 4.4 seconds
  • Top Speed Around 155 mph (250 km/h)
  • Weight (GSR) Approximately 1,360 kg (2,998 lbs)
