Honda CR-V

(2023)

  • Engines: A 1.5-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine (190 horsepower) powers the LX, EX, and EX-L trims, while a 2.0-liter hybrid powertrain (204 total horsepower) is standard on the Sport and Sport Touring trims.
  • Drivetrain: Front-wheel drive (FWD) is standard on most trims, with Honda's Real Time All-Wheel Drive (AWD) available as an option and standard on the top-tier Sport Touring Hybrid.
  • Fuel Economy: Gas-powered FWD models achieve an EPA-estimated 28 city/34 highway MPG, while hybrid FWD models achieve 43 city/36 highway MPG.
  • Interior Space: The CR-V offers a spacious cabin with up to 39.3 cubic feet of cargo space with all seats in use, expanding to an impressive 76.5 cubic feet with the rear seats folded down.
  • Technology: Standard features include a 7-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, with a larger 9-inch touchscreen, wireless charging, and a 12-speaker Bose audio system available on higher trims.
  • Safety: The full Honda Sensing suite of safety and driver-assistive technologies is standard across all trims and includes features like adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking.
