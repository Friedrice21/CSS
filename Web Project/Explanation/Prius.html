Toyota Prius

(2024)

  • Engine : 2.0L 4-cylinder hybrid system

  • Horsepower : 194 hp (FWD) or 196 hp (AWD) combined system output

  • Transmission : Electronically controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (eCVT)

  • Drivetrain : Front-Wheel Drive (FWD) standard, All-Wheel Drive (AWD) available

  • Fuel Economy (Combined) Up to 57 MPG (LE trim FWD)

  • 0-60 mph Time 7.2 seconds (FWD), 7.0 seconds (AWD)

  • Cargo Space Up to 23.8 cubic feet with seats up (LE trim)

Striking Design: The current generation features a low-slung, aerodynamic profile with sharp LED headlights and taillights, moving away from the previously conventional hybrid styling. Advanced Technology: An 8-inch touchscreen is standard, with a larger 12.3-inch Toyota Audio Multimedia display available on higher trims, supporting wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™. Available features also include a digital key and an 8-speaker JBL® premium audio system. Comprehensive Safety: All models come standard with the Toyota Safety Sense™ 3.0 suite, which includes features like pre-collision system with pedestrian detection, adaptive cruise control, lane departure alert with steering assist, and road sign assist. Improved Performance: The current Prius is significantly more powerful than previous generations, offering quicker acceleration without sacrificing its class-leading fuel efficiency. Interior Comfort: The cabin is driver-centric with a high-mounted digital gauge cluster to keep information in the driver's line of sight. It features standard cloth or available SofTex®-trimmed, heated and ventilated front seats, and a fixed glass roof is available on the Limited trim.
